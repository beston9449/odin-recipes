Sushi

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make sushi recipe that's perfect for a quick, satisfying meal or appetizer.

Method

  1. Prepare the sushi rice by rinsing it until the water runs clear, then cook it according to the package instructions. Once cooked, let it cool and season with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Prepare your fillings, such as fresh fish, vegetables, or avocado. Slice them into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the sushi by spreading a thin layer of rice onto a sheet of nori (seaweed), then add the fillings in the center.
  4. Roll the sushi tightly using a bamboo mat, then slice into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
