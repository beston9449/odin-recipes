Potato

Description

A quick and easy recipe for baked potatoes, perfect when you're in a hurry but still want something tasty.

Method

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 400°F (200°C). Wash and dry the potatoes.
  2. Bake: Rub the potatoes with oil and salt. Bake for 45 to 60 minutes, until soft inside and crispy outside.
  3. Prepare Toppings: While the potatoes bake, get your favorite toppings ready, like butter, cheese, or sour cream.
  4. Serve: Remove the potatoes from the oven, slice open, and top with your chosen ingredients. Enjoy!
